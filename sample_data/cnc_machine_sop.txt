# CNC Machine - Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

## 1. Safety Requirements
- Safety glasses and steel-toed shoes must be worn at all times.
- Do not wear loose clothing or jewelry near the spindle.
- Ensure all emergency stop buttons are functional before startup.

## 2. Startup Procedure
1. Turn on the main power disconnect switch located on the electrical cabinet.
2. Wait for the control system to boot (approx. 2-3 minutes).
3. Press the "Machine Power ON" button.
4. Release the Emergency Stop button.
5. Perform a reference point return (Zero Return) for all axes (X, Y, Z).
6. Check coolant levels and ensure the pump is primed.

## 3. Operation
- Load the workpiece securely into the chuck or vise.
- Load the correct tooling into the tool carousel.
- Run a dry pass (no workpiece contact) to verify the tool path.
- Activate coolant and begin the machining cycle.

## 4. Emergency Shutdown Procedure
In case of fire, excessive vibration, or mechanical failure:
1. Hit the EMERGENCY STOP (E-STOP) button immediately.
2. Turn off the main power disconnect.
3. Evacuate the immediate area.
4. Notify the Plant Manager or Safety Officer.
